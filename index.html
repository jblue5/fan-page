Why Jog?

I enjoy jogging first thing in the morning becuase it offers a challenge right after
getting out of bed. In the past I would rely I on energy drinks, shots, or expresso.
It wakes me up and there is a peaceful calm when its all done, when done outside early and
its just you and nature, there isn't a better being "present" moment when you finish the run.
