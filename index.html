The Benefits of Jogging

I hope to jog here

Why Jog?

I enjoy jogging first thing in the morning becuase it offers a challenge right after getting out of bed. In the past I would rely I on energy drinks, shots, or expresso. It wakes me up and there is a peaceful calm when its all done, when done outside early and its just you and nature, there isn't a better being "present" moment when you finish the run.

Preparation

  1. Hydate plenty
  2. gather running gear
  3. Plan route to be run
  4. Set alarm
  5. Tackle the run

Where

Best places to run are next to bodies of water.

  • Nokomis
  • Lake of the Isles
  • Emerald Isle, NC

To get faster, add a sprint regiment to break up monotony:

  • Warm up with 10 minute jog.
  • Sprint in a bursts or distances at 100, 200, 300 or 400 meters.
  • Rest for a few minutes and repeat two more intervals.

Dont forget to stretch after the run
